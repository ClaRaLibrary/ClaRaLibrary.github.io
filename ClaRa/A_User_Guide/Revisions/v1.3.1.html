v1.3.1

Created Montag 05 März 2018

This Template shall be used.

Overview


New Models

  • A new Example model (ClaRa.Examples.SteamPowerPlant_01) of a complex power plant has been added which uses the same steam cycle as example SteamCycle_02 but a more complex boiler model which uses a static cycle model and features gas and particle radiation.
  • A new sensor for measuring the lower heating value in fuel connections was added

User Experience

  • updated icons for pipes making advanced pipes and simple pipes distinguishable
  • updated cons for joins and splits to be in analogy to pipe icons
  • added visualisation for VLE split and join pressure losses (in analogy to the pipes)
  • made some icons more compliant with ClaRa icon conventions and removed level of detail visualiser from icons
  • Added lower heating value to summary of fuel transporting components
  • New quadruple visualiser for StaticCycle models


Bugfixes

  • A_heat from generic heat transfer models removed (now using geo.A_heat). If generic heat transfer models have been used in gas pipes (VolumesValvesFittings.Pipes.PipeFlowGas_L4_Simple) or regenerative air preheaters (HeatExchangers.RegenerativeAirPreheater_L4 and HeatExchangers.RegenerativeAirPreheaterPrimaryAndSecondaryAir_L4) the heat transfer area was not calculated correctly. The specialized heat transfer model for regenerative air preheaters was not affected.
  • A bug was fixed where the usage of generic heat transfer models for the ClaRa.Components.HeatExchangers.RegenerativeAirPreheater_L4 and ClaRa.Components.HeatExchangers.RegenerativeAirPreheaterPrimaryAndSecondaryAir_L4 lead to incorrect heat transfer surfaces
  • Recall of logarithmic mean temperature calculation bugfix of ClaRa v. 1.3.0. The smoothed calculation of Delta_T_log was erroneous which led to higher heat flows than intended. The Heat flow should now be the same for smoothed and unsmoothed calculation of the logarithmic mean temperature for negative values.
  • XYplot: bugfixed dimension of input x2 (previeously, N_nodes1 and N_nodes2 had to be equal)
  • Some gas sensors had fixed reference to SimCenter.FlueGas which has been changed
  • bugfixed summary of gas and vle valves
  • bugfixed ClaRa.StaticCycles.ValvesConnects.Valve_cutPressure1: changed sign of Delta_p_nom
  • bugfixed StaticCycles.HeatExchangers.Preheater_twoShell with respect to used outlet port
